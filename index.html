Predict The Numeral


  Rules of the game:

1. You are given a question and you have to identify the number.

2. If you identify the number with just the question, you'll get 10 points.

3. You can use hints, if you can't figure out the answer.

4. You can use one hints in total. If you use one hint, you can get a maximum of 5 points.

5. After you've used your hint and you still can't figure out the number, then you can reveal the number by clicking on "Get a Hint" button but you'll get 0 points.

6. Please do ensure to click on submit before proceeding to the next question.

7. Else you'll get 0 points for the question.
